    Chartered Engineer

    Chartered Engineers are recognised as professionals with the highest level of engineering competence and expertise. They develop solutions to engineering problems using new or existing technologies, through innovation, creativity and change and/or they may have technical accountability for complex systems with significant levels of risk.
